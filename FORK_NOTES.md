[anhaidgroup](https://github.com/anhaidgroup) (Magellan Data Management Group)
at the University of Wisconsin–Madison.

## Original Research

The Sparkly system was originally published in:

> Paulsen, D., Govind, Y., & Doan, A. (2023). Sparkly: A Simple yet
> Surprisingly Strong TF/IDF Blocker for Entity Matching.
> *Proceedings of the VLDB Endowment*, 16(6), 1507–1519.

Please cite the original paper when using this software in academic work.
